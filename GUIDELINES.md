# Advanced Notepad - Usage Guidelines

Welcome to the **Advanced Notepad**! This guide will help you make the most of the premium features implemented in this application.

## 🚀 Getting Started
When you first open the app, you'll be greeted by our premium animated **Splash Screen**. From there, you'll enter the **Home Screen**, where all your thoughts are synchronized with the cloud via Firebase.

---

## 📸 Photo Attachments (Powered by Cloudinary)
You can now attach high-quality images to your notes! 
1. Open any note or create a new one.
2. Tap the **Image Icon** in the top bar.
3. Select an image from your gallery.
4. Your image will be securely uploaded to **Cloudinary** and synced across your devices.

---

## 👤 User Profile & Customization
Make the app yours by setting up your profile:
1. Open the **Navigation Drawer** (hamburger menu) from the Home Screen.
2. Tap on the **User Avatar** to upload a custom profile picture.
3. Tap on the **Edit Icon** next to your name to change your display name.
4. Your profile information is persistent and will be remembered every time you open the app.

---

## 🎨 Note Organization
- **Color Coding**: Each note can have its own vibrant background color. Use the color toolbar at the bottom of the editor to categorize your thoughts visually.
- **Pinning**: Keep your most important notes at the top by tapping the **Pin Icon**.
- **Archiving**: Declutter your main view by moving old notes to the **Archive**. Access archived notes from the Sidebar menu.

---

## ☁️ Cloud Sync
Everything you write is automatically saved to **Firebase Cloud Firestore**. This ensures your data is safe even if you lose your device.

---

## 📄 About & Versions
Check the **About** section in the Sidebar to see the current app version and developer information. 

Enjoy your premium note-taking experience!
